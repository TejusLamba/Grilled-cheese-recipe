Grilled cheese recipe

Learn how to make a simple grilled cheese

Igredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • cheese

instructions

  1. lay out 2 slices of bread and butter the backside of each piece
  2. Lay the cheese on 1 piece of bread
  3. put the 2 slices together
  4. turn on the stove to medium heat
  5. Put the sandwhich on the pan for 2-3 minutes on each side
